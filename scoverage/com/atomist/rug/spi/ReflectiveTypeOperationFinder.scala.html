3 /**
4   * Uses reflection to export type information about a type.
5   * Types with methods with @ExportFunction annotations should
6   * return this type, passing their own class as argument.
7   *
8   * @param classToExamine class to look for annotated operations on
9   * @see ExportFunction
10   */
